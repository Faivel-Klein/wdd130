Argentina

Argentina is the eighth-largest country in the world and the second-largest in South America after Brazil. The capital and largest city is Buenos Aires, a vibrant metropolis known for its European-style architecture, tango music, and cultural richness. Argentina has a diverse landscape, ranging from the Andes Mountains in the west to the Pampas (fertile plains) and Patagonia in the south.
